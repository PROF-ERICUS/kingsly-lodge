Frequently Asked Questions

How do I book a property?

Simply use the search form to find a property and contact us through the listing details.

Are there additional charges?

No hidden charges. Prices listed are all-inclusive unless stated otherwise.

Can I cancel my booking?

Yes, cancellation policies vary depending on the property. Check the listing details for specific policies.

Do you verify the properties?

Yes, we ensure all properties listed on our platform are verified for authenticity and quality.

How do I contact the property owner?

Property owner contact details are provided on each listing page for easy communication.

What payment methods are accepted?

We accept mobile money, bank transfers, and major credit/debit cards.
